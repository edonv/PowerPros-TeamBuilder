How To Use the Website!
  • Click a player to see their info on the right.
  • Double-click a player to see more detailed info.
  • Right-click a player to add them to your team.
Tips:
  • You can hover the mouse over almost anything and it will give you more information.
  • Click a position at the top of the page to filter the player pool by their primary position.
  • Right-click a position to filter the players by all positions they play.
